Glam by Dang

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how the Glam by Dang Instagram messaging assistant ("the App") handles information it receives while responding to Instagram Direct Messages sent to @glambydangnyc.

Information We Receive

When you send a Direct Message to @glambydangnyc, the App may receive:

  • The text content of your messages
  • Your Instagram-assigned sender ID (a numeric identifier assigned by Meta)
  • The timestamp of your message

We do not receive your Instagram username, profile photo, follower count, or any other profile information.

How We Use This Information

Message content is used solely to generate a relevant, automated reply on behalf of Glam by Dang. Specifically:

  • Messages are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API to generate a response
  • Conversation history is held in memory for up to 24 hours to provide context for follow-up messages, then discarded
  • No message content is written to a database or stored on disk

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic — message content is transmitted to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Meta (Instagram) — messages are received and sent via the Instagram Messaging API under Meta's platform terms.
  • Railway — the App is hosted on Railway's infrastructure. Railway's privacy policy is available at railway.app/legal/privacy.

Data Retention

Conversation data is held in memory only for the duration of an active session (up to 24 hours of inactivity). It is never written to a database or long-term storage. Server logs may retain IP addresses and request metadata for up to 7 days as part of standard infrastructure logging.

Your Rights

You may stop interacting with the assistant at any time by simply not messaging @glambydangnyc. Because no personal data is stored beyond the active session, there is no persistent record to delete. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at glambydang@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
